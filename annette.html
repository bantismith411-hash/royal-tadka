welcome to annette

Annette is a James Beard Award-winning wood-fired American restaurant and cocktail bar located inside Stanley Marketplace in Aurora, Colorado. Named one of America’s best new restaurants by Bon Appétit and Food & Wine, Annette offers a warm, welcoming space to gather, share a meal, and experience ingredient-driven cooking rooted in hospitality.

scratch to table

At Annette, “scratch to table” isn’t just a tagline, it’s the heart of everything we do. We cook from the ground up, honoring the ingredients, the seasons, and the people who grow our food. Every dish starts with real ingredients, shaped by hand, cooked over live fire, and served with intention.

Born from years spent working the land and learning the rhythm of real food, Chef Caroline Glover brings a scratch-made philosophy to every plate. From wood-fired vegetables to handmade desserts, we believe food should tell a story; honest, rustic, and deeply nourishing.

Dining at Annette is like being welcomed into a home: a place where good meals are shared, good stories are told, and memories are made around the table.
